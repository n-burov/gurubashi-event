📖 ▼ The Whispers — Lore

"Yuh no need fi know mi name, brethren call me Fingerpunch. I flick finger, you feel punch..."

Word reach. Alliance gather in Hyjal. Summon Lycan Father. Dem tink nobody watch. We ride. All di way… mi hear whisper. Soft. "Blood…" Mi shake head. Voice come back. Hyjal want blood. Hyjal hungry. Good. Mi drown di soil inna blood. Di voice no need fi tell mi.

Reach Circle of Power… Alliance already fighting worgen beast. Good timing. Fiyah drop pon dem. Lightning crack. Steel bite. Dem line break quick quick. Dem scatter. Dem scream. Dem dead. Ritual mash up. Alliance fail fi kill Lycan. But whisper still dere. Inside mi skull. Between mi ears. Low. Slow. "More blood."

Later… pigskin messenger lose head near Ratchet. Letter inna pocket. Meeting. Bleakhollow Crater. Alliance want fi talk. Want Horde allow ritual next time. Mi choke cackle. Tears in mi eyes, grin pon mi face. Horde Alliance Meeting. Inside volcano crater. Talk like friend. Dem tink mi brethren naive. If dem waan gather… we shall gather. Bring banner. Bring speech. Bring all dem brave hero. Crater waiting. And whisper still talking. Soft. Hungry. Hyjal remember every drop. Hyjal always want more blood.

— Fingerpunch, Kor'Kron Enforcer
