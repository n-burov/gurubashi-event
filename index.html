📖 ▼ Fingerpunch's Tale — Read

"Night sea quiet. Too quiet. Mi float low, only eyes above water. Salt bite mi skin. Voices wake again. 'Gold…' 'Take it…' 'Blood…'"

Mi see it. Ship. Heavy. Slow. Mi swim. Silent. Cold wrap mi body, but mi whisper small magic. Frost numb mi limbs, arcane hush mi movement. No splash. No sound. Mi climb. Deck creak. Guard turn. Too late. Blade slip in. Mi catch him before he drop. Lay him down soft. "More…" Next one get a flicker. Tiny flame inna him face. Him blink. Mi cut him throat while him blind. Blood quiet. Good.

Cabin. Captain counting gold. Mi blink — small arcane shimmer. Him hesitate. Mi smash him skull. Chest mine. Heavy. Voices pleased. "Take it… go… spill more…" Ship Bells, Alarm raised. Mi gone. Overboard. Water swallow mi. Mi sink, hold breath, whisper again. Arcane keep mi lungs calm, body light. Mi swim deep, far. No chase. Only silence. And di voices inna mi head, I trust them voices, I will do what they say. "Arena…" "Hide it…"

So mi go Gurubashi. Dig. Bury de chest. Sand cover secret. Mi stand up, blood still on mi hands, and laugh. Because dem going come. All of dem. For gold. For glory. For death. And when dem fight… When sand drink deep… Mi slip away. Booty Bay waiting. Voices still whisper. "More blood… soon…"

— Fingerpunch, Troll Mage, Kor'Kron Enforcers
